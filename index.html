Spotify Decision Tree

Decision tree visualization for Spotify track popularity analysis.

Understanding the Decision Tree

The decision tree shown above represents the factors that influence the popularity of Spotify tracks. Each node in the tree represents a feature and its threshold value, used to split the data. The final nodes, or leaves, represent the predicted popularity category of the tracks.

Key Features:

  • Instrumentalness: Indicates whether a track is instrumental.
  • Acousticness: Measures the acoustic quality of a track.
  • Danceability: Describes how suitable a track is for dancing.
  • Energy: Represents the intensity and activity of a track.

Tracks with low instrumentalness and low acousticness tend to be more popular. High energy and high danceability also contribute to higher popularity.
